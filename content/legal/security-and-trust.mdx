<LocalizedLegalEnglish>

<Callout type="warning">
RocketAiFlow is not currently presented as a certified SaaS trust center.
</Callout>

## Shared Responsibility

For on-premise deployments, security is shared between RocketAiFlow and the customer.

RocketAiFlow is responsible for the software, documentation, recommended configuration, and product updates it provides.

The customer is responsible for infrastructure, access control, secrets, network security, backups, telephony, provider accounts, logs, retention, and operational monitoring in its own environment.

## Support Access

Support access to customer data or systems occurs only where agreed for troubleshooting or services.

## Responsabilità condivisa

Per deployment on-premise, la sicurezza è condivisa tra RocketAiFlow e cliente.

RocketAiFlow è responsabile del software, della documentazione, della configurazione raccomandata e degli aggiornamenti prodotto forniti.

Il cliente è responsabile di infrastruttura, access control, secret, network security, backup, telephony, account provider, log, retention e monitoring operativo nel proprio ambiente.

## Accesso supporto

L'accesso del supporto a dati o sistemi cliente avviene solo quando concordato per troubleshooting o servizi.
