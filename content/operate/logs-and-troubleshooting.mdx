# Logs and Troubleshooting

Troubleshooting Voice AI workflows requires both conversation context and system context. Teams
need enough evidence to understand what the caller experienced, what the workflow decided, and what
dependencies were doing at the time.

## Useful troubleshooting inputs

- call and workflow identifiers
- decision and routing events
- external lookup or API failures
- transfer or handoff events
- timing data that explains delay or interruption

## A practical investigation flow

1. Confirm the workflow path the call took.
2. Review dependency behavior at each important step.
3. Identify whether the issue was design, context, or runtime failure.
4. Decide whether the fix belongs in workflow logic, integration handling, or operations.
