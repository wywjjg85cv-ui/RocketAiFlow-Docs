# Performance Review

Performance review should connect call behavior to business value. Raw call volume or transcript
quality is not enough. Teams need to know whether the workflow is resolving the right interactions
with acceptable operational efficiency.

## Useful review themes

- containment quality for the intended workflow
- escalation quality for unresolved cases
- task completion or downstream action quality
- consistency across similar call types

## Good review rhythm

Use a repeatable review cadence with both technical and operational ownership. A shared review loop
is what turns call activity into durable workflow improvement.
