# Monitoring and Visibility

RocketAiFlow should be operated with enough visibility to answer both technical and operational
questions. It is not enough to know that calls occurred. Teams need to know how workflows behaved,
where they failed, and where handoff or containment quality needs work.

## What to monitor

- workflow volume and completion outcomes
- handoff frequency and failure patterns
- dependency health across telephony and business systems
- latency or interruption patterns that affect caller experience

## Why it matters

Monitoring turns workflow behavior into something teams can improve. Without it, issues often
surface only through anecdotal feedback or missed business outcomes.
