# Example Use Cases

These examples are meant to show fit, not to imply a fixed vertical strategy.

## Inbound examples

- service intake and pre-qualification
- support triage before specialist routing
- scheduling and confirmation flows
- information capture before human follow-up

## Outbound examples

- lead qualification and follow-through
- reminder and confirmation programs
- account or task status collection
- callback or reactivation workflows
