# Deployment Overview

Deployment choice shapes more than infrastructure. It affects implementation speed, system access,
operational ownership, change control, and how closely the Voice AI layer can align with the rest
of the customer environment.

## Common deployment paths

- **Cloud deployment** for faster rollout and lower infrastructure management overhead
- **On-premise deployment** for tighter infrastructure control and local environment alignment
- **Phased models** where one environment is used first and expanded later

## Tradeoffs to evaluate

- who owns infrastructure and runtime operations
- how quickly environments can be provisioned or changed
- what level of control is needed over data paths and networking
- how telephony and internal systems are exposed to the workflow layer

## Safe planning guidance

Avoid assuming identical backup, disaster recovery, or uptime behavior across deployment models.
Those outcomes depend on the actual environment, the surrounding operational design, and who owns
the underlying infrastructure.
