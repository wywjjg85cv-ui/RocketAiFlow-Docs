# Operational Considerations

Deployment decisions only work when they are backed by clear operational ownership. The question is
not just where RocketAiFlow runs, but who is responsible for observing it, supporting it, and
improving it after launch.

## Areas that need explicit ownership

- environment access and change control
- monitoring and alert review
- incident investigation
- backup and recovery planning
- workflow review and iteration cadence

## Questions to answer before launch

- Who is the first responder for workflow or integration issues?
- Where do logs and call artifacts live?
- How are deployment changes approved and rolled back?
- What is the review process for false handoffs or missed resolutions?

## Why this matters

Voice workflow quality can degrade quietly if nobody owns the review loop. Operational readiness is
part of product readiness for RocketAiFlow.
