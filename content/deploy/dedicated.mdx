# Dedicated

Dedicated delivers a fully managed, single-tenant runtime with complete isolation, compliance
alignment, and region-specific deployment. It gives teams the control of self-hosting without the
DevOps burden.

## Why teams choose this model

Common reasons include:

- faster initial rollout without building and operating the runtime internally
- isolation requirements that still need a managed operating model
- compliance alignment and region-specific deployment requirements
- a cleaner path to observability, support, and day-two operations with lower internal overhead

## What to validate

Before launch, confirm:

- telephony connectivity is clearly understood
- provider choice has been validated against the deployment and configuration model
- external API actions can be secured and monitored
- operators have one reliable path into dashboards, logs, and traces
- support ownership is clear between the RocketAiFlow team and the customer team
- the first launch scope is small enough for the support team to manage

## Where teams usually get into trouble

Dedicated deployments often fail operationally when:

- the managed runtime is assumed to remove the need for telephony validation
- monitoring is added too late
- API dependencies are treated as background details
- campaign scale increases before call review and observability are trusted
