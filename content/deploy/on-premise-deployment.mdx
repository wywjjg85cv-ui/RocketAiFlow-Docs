# On-Premise Deployment

On-premise deployment is usually chosen when infrastructure control, local system access, or
environmental requirements matter more than rollout speed.

## What teams usually gain

- more control over where the platform runs
- closer fit with internal network boundaries
- clearer alignment with local telephony or business systems
- more control over operational change management

## What teams usually own

- infrastructure provisioning and maintenance
- runtime access, observability, and incident response
- backup and recovery design
- environment patching and rollout process

## What to validate early

- network access between telephony, workflow services, and business systems
- operational responsibility after launch
- upgrade and rollback expectations
- how logs and troubleshooting data will be collected and reviewed
