# Telephony and System Fit

RocketAiFlow should fit the surrounding telephony and system environment before the workflow is
treated as production-ready. This is where many otherwise solid demos break down.

## Assess the telephony edge

Review how calls enter the workflow, how routing happens, what information is available at answer
time, and how handoff or transfer should behave under real conditions.

## Assess internal system fit

Review how customer, task, or case context is fetched, what systems must be updated after the
interaction, and what failure behavior is acceptable when those systems are unavailable.

## Key questions

- how will SIP, PBX, or carrier paths connect into the workflow?
- what context is available at the start of the call?
- what happens if a lookup fails mid-call?
- what does a successful handoff look like for the receiving team?
