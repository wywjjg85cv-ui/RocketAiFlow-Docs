# Asterisk Monitoring

RocketAiFlow can include pre-provisioned Asterisk dashboards so teams have immediate visibility
into telephony behavior without needing to design the first Asterisk views from scratch.

The two most important starting points are `Asterisk Log` and `Asterisk Overview`.

## Asterisk Log

Use `Asterisk Log` for telephony-related log activity.

It helps teams review:

- log events connected to live call behavior
- warnings and errors that affect routing or call handling
- telephony-side events during troubleshooting

Use it when the issue feels specific, event-driven, or easier to understand through log context.

## Asterisk Overview

Use `Asterisk Overview` for higher-level Asterisk operational monitoring.

It helps teams review:

- service-level telephony behavior
- overall Asterisk operating state
- higher-level activity patterns before going into logs

Use it when you want a broader operational picture before drilling down into log-level details.

## When teams usually use these dashboards

- Start with `Asterisk Overview` when validating telephony health at a higher level.
- Move to `Asterisk Log` when you need event-level investigation or log review.

## What kinds of issues they help investigate

- unexpected telephony behavior
- call-path instability
- routing symptoms that may actually belong to the Asterisk layer
- incident windows where logs and service behavior need to be correlated
