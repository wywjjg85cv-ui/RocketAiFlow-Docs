# Workflow Design

Workflow design in RocketAiFlow should begin with the operating model, not with the script. The
best first question is: what business result should this call produce, and what should happen when
that result is not available?

## A practical design checklist

- define the workflow objective in one sentence
- identify the required inputs at call time
- map the key decision points
- define success, partial success, and failure outcomes
- define the recovery or handoff path

## Keep the first version narrow

A narrow workflow is easier to validate, easier to monitor, and easier to improve. Start with the
most common case and the most important edge cases. Avoid combining unrelated intents into a single
flow just because they share a telephony entry point.

## What to document alongside the workflow

- who owns the workflow after launch
- what systems it reads from and writes to
- what events should be logged
- what review process exists for exceptions and drift
