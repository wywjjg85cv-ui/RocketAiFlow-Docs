# Platform-Agnostic Architecture

RocketAiFlow is documented as platform-agnostic by design. That means the platform is intended to
fit different infrastructure choices across telephony, speech recognition, voice generation, model
inference, and connected systems depending on the implementation.

## Architectural principle

Separate the workflow logic from the provider-specific execution layers wherever practical. This
makes it easier to adapt the stack for customer requirements without rewriting the core workflow
itself.

## Benefits of a platform-agnostic approach

- better fit for different customer environments
- less dependency on one provider decision
- easier adaptation to performance, cost, or compliance requirements
- clearer separation between business workflow logic and infrastructure choice

## What this means in practice

The workflow should remain understandable even if the telephony provider, speech layer, or model
layer changes. That requires stable contracts for inputs, outputs, error handling, and fallback
behavior across the system.
