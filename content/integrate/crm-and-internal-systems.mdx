# CRM and Internal Systems

Business systems provide the context that makes Voice AI useful in operations. They also receive the
results of the interaction, whether that is a note, a task, a status update, or a routed outcome.

## Common roles for internal systems

- caller or account lookup
- case, lead, or task context
- scheduling and availability checks
- outcome recording after the interaction

## Integration rule of thumb

Only expose the data and actions the workflow truly needs. Over-connecting the workflow to internal
systems can make testing, failure handling, and access control harder than necessary.
