# Telephony / SIP / PBX

Telephony is the operational edge of the workflow. It determines how calls enter the system, what
context is available at answer time, and how transfers, queues, or fallback paths behave.

## Integration concerns

- inbound and outbound call entry points
- call routing and transfer behavior
- caller or destination metadata passed into the workflow
- compatibility with existing PBX, SIP, or carrier logic

## Why it matters

Even a strong workflow will feel broken if routing context is incomplete or if handoff behavior is
inconsistent. Telephony fit should be validated early, especially when existing routing logic is
already complex.
