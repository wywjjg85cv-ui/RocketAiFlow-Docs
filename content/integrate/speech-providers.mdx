# Speech Providers

The speech layer turns live caller audio into usable workflow input. Its role is not only
transcription quality. It also affects latency, interruption handling, and how confidently the
workflow can move from caller input to business action.

## Evaluation criteria

- accuracy for the domain and caller profile
- latency under realistic call conditions
- handling of noisy or accented audio
- operational fit with the deployment model

## Documentation guidance

Provider-specific setup should be documented once the supported integration paths are confirmed. At
the product level, the important point is that RocketAiFlow can fit different speech layers as part
of a broader implementation.
