# Voice / TTS Providers

The TTS layer shapes how the workflow sounds to the caller. It influences intelligibility, pace,
brand fit, and the overall feel of the interaction.

## What to evaluate

- clarity and naturalness for the target call type
- response speed during live interactions
- voice consistency across different scenarios
- support for the deployment and integration model being used

## Practical note

This layer should be chosen in the context of the workflow. A voice that sounds good in isolation
is not enough if it introduces latency or makes the operational flow harder to review and improve.
